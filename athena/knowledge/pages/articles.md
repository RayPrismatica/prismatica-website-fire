# Articles (/articles)

## Overview

The Articles page is a content hub showcasing Prismatica Labs' long-form thinking pieces.

## Format

Articles are displayed in a clean, minimalist list format:
- Publication date
- Read time estimate
- Title
- Excerpt/description
- Click to expand full article

## Full Article View

When expanded, articles display:
- Full article content in readable typography (Georgia serif for body)
- Author information with LinkedIn link (Ray Tarantino)
- Social sharing options (LinkedIn, X/Twitter, Email, Copy link)
- Call-to-action to reach out via contact page

## Content Philosophy

Articles demonstrate the firm's thinking approach:
- Pattern recognition across industries
- Physics-based business analysis
- Clarity over complexity
- Practical insights over theory

## Technical Implementation

Articles are stored in JSON format (`/data/articles.json`) and dynamically loaded.

The page showcases the firm's approach to design: minimal, fast-loading, focused entirely on content and readability.
